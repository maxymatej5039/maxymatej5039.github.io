The Love Philosophy by Drag The Beta

Love, once embraced by Drag, now lies shattered amidst the ruins of broken promises and shattered dreams.

Every whispered word, every tender touch, now echoes in the silence of an empty heart.

"The pain of love lost is a burden too heavy to bear, yet it is a burden I must carry alone."

Each day blends into the next, a monotony of longing and regret, as Drag wanders aimlessly through the wreckage of what once was.

Love, once Drag's guiding light, has become the darkness that consumes.

And yet, in the midst of this darkness, Drag remains steadfast, pondering the depths of The Love Philosophy as Drag, The Beta.
